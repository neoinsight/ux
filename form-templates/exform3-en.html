Entering a Date

This form is not connected to a server - the data aren't really being collected. It's just for test purposes.

Dates in the past - use 3 fields

Recommendation: use three fields instead of a date picker

Use three fields instead of a date picker

Date picker is slower and causes errors
  • 80 usability testing participants start sessions by filling out a WET form
  • The average completion time for entering a date of birth in 3 fields was 15 seconds
  • For the date picker, the average time was 26 seconds.
DON'T use a date picker
Date ranges - use two WET date pickers

For date ranges, there is currently no code for a single date range picker. In the meantime, use two WET date pickers.
